About Jaipur Foot

Jaipur Foot, through the Bhagwan Mahaveer Viklang Sahayata Samiti (BMVSS), has transformed millions of lives by offering free prosthetic limbs, calipers, crutches, and other aids to persons with disabilities. Established with a vision of serving the underprivileged, the organization has become the world's largest service provider in the field of physical rehabilitation, recognized globally for its innovation, affordability, and compassion.

The iconic 'Jaipur Foot' prosthetic is not only low-cost and durable but also designed to adapt to the diverse lifestyles and working conditions of beneficiaries, from walking on uneven rural terrain to squatting and sitting cross-legged.

Our Mission Together

In partnership with Jaipur Foot, the world's largest organization providing free prosthetic limbs and mobility aids, we aim to empower persons with disabilities by restoring their independence and dignity.

Innovation & Compassion

The organization's success lies in its unique combination of technological innovation and human compassion. Each prosthetic is custom-fitted and designed to meet the specific needs of the individual, ensuring not just mobility but also the ability to work, play, and live with dignity.

Through our partnership, Project UDAAN leverages Jaipur Foot's expertise to provide comprehensive support to our beneficiaries, ensuring they receive the best possible care and assistance in their journey towards independence and success.
